Who I Am

I am Raymond Tembo — a teacher, school administrator, and preacher based in Bindura, Zimbabwe. Rural Enterprise

Farming & Livestock

Based near Bindura, we run a small-scale rural enterprise combining livestock and market gardening — built for long-term food security and income generation.

🐐 Goat Farming

Indigenous goats raised for meat and sale. Hardy, low-maintenance, and well-suited to the local environment. Goats provide a reliable income stream and grow the herd as capital builds.

🐔 Road Runner Chickens

Free-range road runner chickens raised naturally on local feed. High demand in local markets due to their taste and quality. Low input costs make this an ideal enterprise alongside goats.

🥬 Market Gardening

Vegetables grown for local sale — the fast cash-generating arm of the enterprise. Tomatoes, leafy greens, and seasonal crops targeting Bindura and nearby community markets.
